Non-commercial permit

For a non-commercial activity involving a group of 75 or more people, either as participants or spectators with no entry/participation fee. The primary purpose of the event isn’t selling goods or services. This type of permit is free.

Temporary outfitter’s permit

For outfitters & guides, temporary use permits allow short-term, non-renewable outfitting and guiding in increments of 50 service days, up to a maximum of 200 service days in a 180-day period. To learn more about a temporary outfitter's permit click here.
