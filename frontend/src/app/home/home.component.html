Apply for a permit

If you know which permit you need, click on the permit link below to log in or create an account using login.gov then fill out and submit that permit. If you’re not sure which permit is the right permit for you, click on the “Help me find the right permit type” button and we’ll guide you to the correct permit.

Non-commercial permit

  • Activity involving a group of 75 or more people, either as participants or spectators.
  • No entry/participation fee.
  • The primary purpose of the event isn’t selling goods or services.
  • This type of permit is free.

Click here to learn more about this permit.

Temporary outfitter’s permit

  • For outfitters & guides.
  • Allows short-term, non-renewable outfitting and guiding.
  • Used in increments of 50 service days.
  • Valid for up to 200 days in a 180-day period per use area.
