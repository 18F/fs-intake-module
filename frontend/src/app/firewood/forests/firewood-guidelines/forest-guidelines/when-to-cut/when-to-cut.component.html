When to cut firewood

Firewood permits are available through the calendar year. However, firewood cutting is not allowed at certain times of year in parts of the forest.

See prohibited areas and restrictions for more information.

Cost

Firewood permits are $20.00 for 4 cords of wood. All permits are nonrefundable.

Buy a permit

You can buy firewood permits online or in-person at one of our district offices.
