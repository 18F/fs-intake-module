When to cut firewood {{forest.forestAbbr}}

Firewood permits are valid through December 31, 2020.

See prohibited areas and restrictions for more information.

Cost

Firewood permits are $5.00 per cord with a 4 cord ($20) minimum purchase. Limit of 12 cords per household per year. All permits are nonrefundable.

Buy a permit

You can buy firewood permits online or in-person at one of our district offices.
