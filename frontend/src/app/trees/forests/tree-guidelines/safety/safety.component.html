• District and Forest maps are available for purchase at any Mt. Hood District Office.
  • Do not solely rely on your vehicle GPS. Some routes may be snowed out depending on the weather.
  • No Cell Phone Coverage on the Clackamas River District
  • Very Limited Cell Phone Coverage on the Zigzag Ranger District
  • Be sure that someone at home knows where you are, and when to expect you back!

REMEMBER!

  • Pick up your permit early - Find your tree and leave the woods before dark
  • Roads are not plowed - Carry tire chains, shovel (s) & tow chain
  • Bring an overnight survival kit in case you become stranded
  • Dress warmly and take extra dry clothes, bring hot drinks and extra food.
