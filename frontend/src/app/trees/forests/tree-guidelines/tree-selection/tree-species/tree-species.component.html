Our National Forests have an abundance of tree species for you to choose from when you’re ready harvest your Christmas tree. As you search the forest for your tree, you’ll see well-known Firs, less-popular Hemlocks – and even varieties that are protected from cutting. The trees will have a natural appearance, as they have not been professionally pruned. Learning about the available trees before you leave on your trip can assist your search for the perfect tree.
