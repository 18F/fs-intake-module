Our National Forests have an abundance of tree species for you to choose from when you’re ready to harvest your Christmas tree. As you search the forest, you’ll see trees that are not trimmed or pruned but instead have been free to grow naturally. Learning about the available trees in your nearby National Forest can help you find the perfect tree.
