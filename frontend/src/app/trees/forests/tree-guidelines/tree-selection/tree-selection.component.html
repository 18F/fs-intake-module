Our National Forests have an abundance of tree species for you to choose from when you’re ready to harvest your Christmas tree. As you search the forest, you’ll see trees that are not trimmed or pruned but instead are in their natural state. Learning about the available trees in the wild, before you leave on your trip, can help you find the perfect tree.
