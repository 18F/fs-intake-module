Firewood permits are $20.00 for 4 cords of wood. All permits are nonrefundable.

You can buy firewood permits online or in-person at one of our district offices.

All dead and down trees may be gathered. Live trees may NOT be cut.

Standing dead trees may be cut only if they are within 200 feet of a legal public road.

Cutting and gathering is only allowed on areas open to cutting and gathering on the firewood maps.

You can download a firewood map below, or pick one up at one of our district offices.
