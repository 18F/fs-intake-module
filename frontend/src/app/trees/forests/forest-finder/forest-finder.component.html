1. Christmas tree permits

Buy a Christmas tree permit online

National Forests have their own rules, season dates and permit prices for cutting down Christmas trees. Not all forests offer a Christmas tree cutting season. For those that do, you can buy your Christmas tree permits in person at the forest ranger district offices. Some forests offer the option to purchase Christmas tree permits online.
