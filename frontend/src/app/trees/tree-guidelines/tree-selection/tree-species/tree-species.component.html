For many homes, the holiday season would not be complete without a fresh scented Christmas tree. Although a Christmas tree permit allows you to cut anywhere on the National Forest, it can sometimes be difficult to pick the right one. Among the more popular trees are true Firs (Noble and Grand), Pine and Douglas-firs. These trees have a more “natural” look with than the intensively cultured trees that may come from a tree farm.
